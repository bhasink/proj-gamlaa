About Us

At the heart of Gamlaa is a quiet but powerful conviction: that environments become more alive, more human, when nature informs their design. This conviction has guided our journey from early prototypes to integrated biophilic systems. An evolution marked by ideas, milestones, and a shared commitment to designing with nature.

Vision

To lead the way in shaping a world where nature-led design is the norm.

Mission

We bring nature into everyday spaces through design and service that is personal. This in turn creates an environment that soothes and inspires.

Purpose

To complete spaces in a way that feels human, through thoughtful integration of nature and a genuine commitment to care.

Timeline of how we have evolved

2013–2018 :

The Beginning

Started as an e-commerce venture aiming to scale, while acquiring 7–8 corporate clients in indoor landscaping.

2018–2020 :

The Pivot

We pivoted fully to workspace greenery, expanding across Hyderabad and establishing our first manufacturing facility in Pune.

2020–2021 :

Purpose Meets Moment

The pandemic revealed the natural role nature could play. We deepened our focus on biophilic design and workplace wellness.

2021–2022 :

Scaling Expertise

We expanded to Bengaluru, Pune, and Mumbai, building end-to-end capabilities to serve growing demand.

2022–2023 :

Going National

Chennai and NCR joined our footprint. Regional partnerships evolved into national relationships as our infrastructure matured.

2023–2024 :

Deepening Roots

Revenue growth reflected what mattered most—long-term client relationships and infrastructure to serve them exceptionally.

2025–2026 :

Opening New Doors

Scaled our manufacturing facility to 35,000 sq ft in Pune, expanding our capacity to craft bespoke biophilic solutions at scale.

Established a full‑fledged innovation lab—bringing together concept development, product design, engineering, and technical expertise under one roof.

Launched our first experience center in Bengaluru, creating a space where design vision meets living reality.

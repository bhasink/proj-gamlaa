India's first large-scale interior vivarium: a living, breathing ecosystem within a corporate workplace

The Challenge

Amazon wanted something unprecedented in their Hyderabad campus—a vivarium that would serve as both workplace sanctuary and design statement. They envisioned an immersive environment where the boundary between work and nature dissolves, but nothing at that scale had been attempted in India before.

Our Approach

We engineered a complete biophilic ecosystem: climate control, live moss walls, humidity management, and a meticulously curated plant composition. From conceptualization through ongoing maintenance, we ensured the vivarium wouldn't just survive—it would thrive and evolve seasonally.

The Result

The vivarium became Amazon's most-talked-about space. Employees describe it as restorative in ways they can't quite articulate. It's a moment of genuine connection to living nature within a high-intensity work environment—proof that scale and sophistication don't diminish that human need.
